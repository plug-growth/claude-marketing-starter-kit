# Setting this up in the Claude app (no terminal, no tech) 💛

This is the easy way. About 10 minutes, once.

---

## Step 1 — Get the Claude app
- Download the **Claude** app for your computer (Mac or Windows) from
  **claude.ai/download**. Install it and sign up. *(There's also a browser version at
  claude.ai if you ever need it, but the app is easiest — just use that.)*
- You'll want the **Pro plan** (~£17/month) so you can make Projects and upload files.

## Step 2 — Download this kit as a folder
- Go to the kit page: **https://github.com/plug-growth/claude-marketing-starter-kit**
- Click the green **`< > Code`** button → **Download ZIP**.
- Open/unzip the downloaded file. You'll get a folder with these files inside:
  - `ABOUT-MY-BUSINESS.md`
  - a `social-post-planner` file
  - a `website-instagram-review` file
  - a `notes-to-actions` file
  - (don't worry about the rest)

## Step 3 — Fill in your business (5 mins, do this once)
- Open **`ABOUT-MY-BUSINESS.md`** (it opens in any text editor / Notes) and fill it in.
  Bullet points are fine. This is what makes Claude sound like *you*.

## Step 4 — Make a Project in Claude
- In Claude, click **Projects** → **Create Project**. Call it something like
  *"My Marketing Helper"*.
- In the project, find **Add files / Project knowledge** and upload all the files from
  the folder (including your filled-in `ABOUT-MY-BUSINESS.md`).

## Step 5 — Paste in the instructions
- In the project, find **Set instructions** (sometimes "Custom instructions") and paste
  this in exactly:

> You are my friendly marketing helper for my small business. You have three jobs,
> described in the uploaded files: planning my social posts, reviewing my website and
> Instagram, and turning my notes into a clear to-do list. Always read
> ABOUT-MY-BUSINESS first so you understand my business and sound like me. When I ask
> for something, follow the matching file's instructions. Keep everything in plain
> English, warm and honest, no marketing jargon, and remember: less is more — don't
> overwhelm me.

## Step 6 — Use it
Just talk to it in the project, normally:
- *"Help me plan my Instagram posts for next week — I've got a pop-up Saturday."*
- *"Have a look at my website [your link] and tell me what's not working."*
- *"Here are my notes from the last few months — what should I actually do first?"*

That's it. Push back, ask for shorter/warmer, treat it like a marketing mate sat next
to you. Any trouble — message Maisie. 💛
