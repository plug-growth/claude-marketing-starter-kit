# Claude Marketing Starter Kit 💛

A little set of Claude "skills" to help you run the marketing side of your business
without it eating your life. Made for you by Maisie.

There are three:

| Skill | What it does | Just say… |
|-------|--------------|-----------|
| 📅 **Social post planner** | Plans + writes your Instagram/social posts | *"Help me plan my posts for next week"* |
| 🔍 **Website & Instagram review** | Honest review of your site + IG, with fixes | *"Can you review my website?"* |
| ✅ **Notes → actions** | Turns your big pile of notes into a clear to-do list | *"Here are my notes, turn them into a list"* |

You don't need to remember the skill names — just ask Claude in plain English and it
picks the right one.

> **Not techy? Start here →** open **[APP-SETUP.md](APP-SETUP.md)** for the easy,
> no-terminal way to set this up in the Claude app. 💛

---

## Step 1 — Fill in your business (5 mins, do this once)

Open **`ABOUT-MY-BUSINESS.md`** and fill it in. Bullet points are fine. This is what
makes everything Claude writes sound like *you* instead of generic marketing waffle.

## Step 2 — Use it

Open this folder in Claude, then just talk to it normally:

- *"What should I post on Instagram this week? I've got a pop-up on Saturday."*
- *"Have a look at my website [your link] and tell me what's not working."*
- *"Here's a load of notes I've been making — what should I actually do first?"*

That's it. Ask follow-up questions, push back, tell it to make things shorter or warmer.
Treat it like a marketing person sitting next to you.

---

## A few tips

- **Be honest with it.** If a draft doesn't sound like you, say so — "too salesy",
  "make it warmer", "I'd never say 'elevate'". It'll fix it.
- **Start small.** Don't try to do everything at once. One skill, one job.
- **Come back and edit `ABOUT-MY-BUSINESS.md`** whenever your business changes — the
  better it knows you, the better it gets.
